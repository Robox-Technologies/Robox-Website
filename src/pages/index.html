Meet Ro/Box

Ro/Box is an ultra-affordable robotics kit designed to make STEM education accessible to any student, anywhere. Whether you’re a budding engineer or a curious coder, Ro/Box offers hands-on experience in building, programming, and problem-solving.

About us

Build it. Code it. Bring it to life.

There is more to Ro/Box than meets the eye. Each kit is jam-packed with features (as much as our budget allows) to give you the most value out of an already affordable product.

  • Plywood chassis: We use sustainable material that looks both elegant and strong to create the box shape. (After all, it wouldn’t be Ro/Box without Box!)
  • Reusable components: No proprietary hardware! All of the components within a Ro/Box kit can be modified, substituted, or used in other robotics projects.
  • Open-sourced: We mean it. Everything from the Ro/Box design schematics, to this website, is open-sourced. It’s our way of giving to the community.
